Keep Track Of Your Bills

MountainUs has a handy bill tracker that will notify you when your bills are close to being due. You also have the option of setting up auto-pay, right in the app!

Keep Your Shopping Habits In Check!

MountainUs keeps track of your shopping habits and creates personalized budgets that allow you to cut back on excess spending.

Save When You Spend!

MountainUs allows you to round up your change each time you spend and place it in a savings account. You can also specify a percentage of your income to be palced in the savings account too!
